NurseConnect is a National Department of Health (NDoH) initiative. It is the one stop shop where nurses can find information, ask for expert help, receive emotional support and have a line of direct communication with the National Department of Health. It utilises mobile software to give nurses in South Africa access to clinical, psychosocial and learning content.

NurseConnect sends messages over WhatsApp and SMS to help nurses with the important work they do. The NurseConnect mobisite, nurseconnect.org, offers a content repository that can be accessed by nurses at any time. There is also a NurseConnect Helpdesk. Nurses can reply to any NurseConnect message over WhatsApp or SMS with a question and a professional will reply.

What is the Refer-a-Friend campaign?

The Refer-a-Friend campaign is a drive to recruit more nurses to sign up to NurseConnect.

Those nurses can access the link and register directly for NurseConnect.
